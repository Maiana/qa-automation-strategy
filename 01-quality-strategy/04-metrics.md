# Quality Metrics

Quality is measured through both engineering and product indicators.

---

## Engineering Metrics

### Flakiness Rate

Percentage of unstable automated tests.

### Mean Time to Detect (MTTD)

Time between defect introduction and detection.

### Mean Time to Resolve (MTTR)

Time required to fix identified defects.

### Regression Execution Time

Total time required to execute full regression.

---

## Product Metrics

### Defect Escape Rate

Percentage of defects detected in production.

### Production Incident Rate

Number of incidents impacting users.

### Risk Coverage Percentage

Coverage aligned to business-critical features rather than total test count.

---

## Metric Philosophy

Metrics are used to drive improvement, not to measure individual performance.
